The results shown in the tables are the average AUROC values of 9 runs on the trajectories generated by a certain graph. For example, graph ID "BN @ 15" denotes the graph type of Brain Networks with 15 nodes. We note that we have three repetitions of graphs for each graph ID. So the results are the average of three runs on each repetition.
